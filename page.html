Fairy Tales

A Bond of Ice and Sunshine

by Sarah Meran

A Bond of Ice and Sunshine is a charming fairy tale about the unlikely friendship between a frost fairy from the Winter Realm and a sun sprite from the Summer Lands. Despite their contrasting natures, they join forces to restore balance to their worlds when a powerful curse threatens to plunge both realms into endless darkness. This heartwarming story celebrates unity, courage, and the magic of embracing differences.

About the Author

Sarah Beran

Sarah Beran is an American author known for her clean, cozy fantasy and fairy tale retellings. She works as an elementary music teacher and has published fourteen books, including The Silent Night and To Crack a Soldier. Her stories are appreciated for their relatable characters and wholesome content.
